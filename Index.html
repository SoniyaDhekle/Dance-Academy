Feel The Beat

Feel The Beat mission is to create lasting systemic change in the performing arts. Not only by bolstering young Black dance students, but also by making sure young artists who are striving for professional dance careers receive the support they need to make sure their aspirations become their realities. At Dance In Color, we want to ensure that students are exposed to dance teachers and choreographers from a variety of racial and ethnic backgrounds. We want to hold performing art studios accountable. Ones day, we dream to expand the change we want to see beyond dance and to other forms of performing arts to create lasting change throughout the entire industry.

About Us

We work to be sure that Black students who are unable to train in their art form due to the expensive nature of pursuing this field can receive funding in order to procure training by attending top notch studios throughout California. Our hope is to change the face of performing arts as we know it. Creating equity in the world of performing arts is paramount to our endeavors at Dance in Color. The Misty Copelands, Audra McDonalds, and Jonathan Bailey Hollands of the world have made it possible for children of all races to ‘Dream in Color’.

Their many projects also include an 8-hour anthology of the Vedas recited by priests of Varanasi and South India distributed in DVDs featuring sub-titles in ten global languages.
